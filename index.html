Qassim Family Medicine Symposium 2025

We are pleased to announce the launch of the "Qassim Family Medicine Symposium 2025," which will be held on April 11 and 12. This Symposium serves as a distinguished gathering for healthcare professionals, aiming to enhance knowledge and exchange ideas in the fields of family medicine and primary care.

The Symposium will host more than 30 prominent speakers from across Saudi Arabia, who will share the latest research and studies in this vital field. Participants will also have the opportunity to benefit from 4 innovative workshops covering diverse topics in an interactive manner, as well as explore over 40 research posters, enriching the educational experience and encouraging innovation in patient care.

We invite all those interested in family medicine and primary care to join us at this exceptional event. This is a great opportunity for networking, building new partnerships, and contributing to the enrichment of scientific and technical content in the healthcare field.

Chairman’s Statement

Welcome to the Qassim Family Medicine Symposium, a platform dedicated to advancing primary healthcare and strengthening the role of family medicine in our communities.

Our objective is to enhance family medicine through evidence-based practices, interdisciplinary collaboration, and innovative solutions. This Symposium brings together experts, researchers, and practitioners to share knowledge, address challenges, and improve patient-centered care.

I extend my sincere gratitude to our speakers, partners, and organizing committees for their dedication. Your contributions are invaluable in shaping the future of healthcare in Qassim and beyond.

We look forward to insightful discussions and meaningful connections. Thank you for joining us.

Dr. Tameem Abdulaziz Alhomaid, MBBS, SBFM, JBFM, Mental Health fellowship Chairman, Qassim Family Medicine Symposium
